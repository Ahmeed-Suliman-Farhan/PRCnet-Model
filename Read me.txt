This is the TensorFlow source code implemented on Jupyter for the paper,  
"PRCnet Model for Automatic Detection of Brain Tumor in MRI Images".
The database must be downloaded and divided into three sections training, validation and testing. 
The dataset can be downloaded from the following link
https://www.kaggle.com/masoudnickparvar/brain-tumor-mri-dataset
